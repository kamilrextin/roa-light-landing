Why Pipeline Stalls.

The Agency Churn Loop

Hire agency. Launch campaigns. Report MQLs. Pipeline stalls. Sales gets blamed. Agency gets replaced.

What's actually happening: No one can explain how paid media connects to revenue — so decisions get made on surface metrics.

What we diagnose: Where leads drop, stall, or get misclassified between ad click and closed revenue.

Channel-Only Thinking

Most agencies optimize platforms in isolation. CTRs improve. CPL goes down. Revenue impact stays unclear.

What's actually happening: Execution looks "fine" inside ad accounts, but downstream leakage is invisible.

What we diagnose: Performance by channel and funnel stage — where optimization is helping vs hiding problems.

Missing Revenue Architecture

Marketing, CRM, and attribution were never designed as a system. Leads exist. Revenue clarity doesn't.

What's actually happening: Attribution is a black box, reporting is fragmented, and leadership can't trust the numbers.

What we diagnose: How data flows from ad platform → CRM → revenue, and what's breaking along the way.

This diagnostic exists to answer one question: "If we spend another dollar, do we know where it will — or won't — turn into revenue?"
